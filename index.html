One workforce.

Some agents talk with your team. Others work silently in the background. You get both.

Conversational

Agents your team and customers chat with in real time — support, research, internal copilots.

Headless

Agents triggered by an event or a schedule. No chat — they just run, on time, every time.

Human-in-the-loop by design. Agents escalate, pause when uncertain, and keep your team in control of every critical decision.
